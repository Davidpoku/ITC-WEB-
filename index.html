ESSCA

 

Founded in 1909 in Angers, France, ESSCA is a grande école specializing in business, as well as a member of the prestigious Conférence des Grandes Ecoles. It offers a five-year diploma – accredited by the French Ministry of Higher Education as a master’s degree – accessible immediately after the baccalauréat or secondary school. Currently with locations in Angers, Paris, Budapest and Shanghai, ESSCA contacted IT Consultis to develop a unique WordPress theme with a strong international focus, to revamp their 300+ pages website.
